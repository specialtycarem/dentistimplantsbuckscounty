FAQ's

A dental implant periodontist is a specialist trained in the gums, bone, and supporting structures around your teeth. This expertise allows us to evaluate your oral health, place implants with precision, and manage healing to give your new teeth the best long-term outcome.

Dental implant cost varies based on the number of teeth being replaced, the complexity of your case, and whether additional procedures like bone grafting are needed. During your consultation, we'll provide a personalized teeth implants price estimate along with financing options.

All on Four is a full-arch restoration technique that replaces an entire row of missing or failing teeth using just four implants. It's an excellent option for patients seeking a fixed, permanent solution without the need for a removable denture. We'll assess your bone health and oral condition to confirm if you're a good candidate.

Recovery timelines vary by patient, but most people return to normal daily activities within a few days, while complete healing and integration of the implants with the jawbone can take a few months. Our team provides detailed aftercare instructions to support a smooth recovery.

Coverage for dental implants varies widely by insurance plan. Our team will help you understand your benefits and explore financing options to make your treatment as affordable as possible.
